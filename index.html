OpenClaw

Private TikTok analytics dashboard

OpenClaw is a private web dashboard that connects to authorized TikTok accounts and displays profile stats, video performance, and analytics data inside a CRM or internal reporting workflow.

What it does

  • Connects securely through TikTok authorization
  • Displays account and video performance data
  • Supports internal reporting and CRM visibility
  • Intended for authorized business and internal use
