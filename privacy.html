OpenClaw Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 16, 2026

OpenClaw connects to TikTok only after user authorization and accesses account data necessary to display analytics and performance data inside a private CRM dashboard.

1. Data We May Collect

  • TikTok profile information
  • TikTok video information and performance metrics
  • Authentication-related tokens provided by TikTok
  • Basic technical information needed to operate the dashboard

2. How We Use Data

  • To display analytics inside the dashboard
  • To support CRM reporting and internal insights
  • To maintain authorized account connections
  • To improve dashboard functionality and reliability

3. Data Sharing

OpenClaw does not sell personal data to third parties. Data is only used for the intended dashboard functionality and authorized internal use.

4. Data Storage and Security

Authorized data and tokens should be stored securely using reasonable administrative, technical, and organizational safeguards.

5. Third-Party Services

OpenClaw may rely on third-party services, including TikTok APIs and infrastructure providers, to operate the dashboard.

6. Contact

For privacy questions, replace and publish your real contact email before submitting: your@email.com
